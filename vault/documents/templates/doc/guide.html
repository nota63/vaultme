Document Upload and Security Guide

Step-by-Step Guide to Uploading Documents
Step 1: Click the upload icon to choose your document.
Step 2: Fill in the required fields in the upload form, such as the document name, type, country of origin, and nominee details.
Step 3: Click the submit button to upload your document. Ensure the document is within the allowed file size and type.
Step 4: Your document will be displayed in the list below where you can view, download, or delete it.
Document Security Tips
Tip 1: Ensure you are logged in before uploading documents. This ensures only authorized users can access your documents.
Tip 2: Only share your documents with trusted parties. Avoid sharing links or files through insecure channels.
Tip 3: Use strong passwords to protect your account. Enable two-factor authentication if available.
Tip 4: Regularly update your documents and remove outdated ones to minimize risks.
